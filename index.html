About us 📝

PANTHPLOW MURO AGENCIES LTD we are a brand in the real estate sector whereby we specialize in buying and selling of land in various parts of the country. We seek to empower those who want to become property owners.We sell genuine land to our customers with clean and legitimate Title deeds. We offer plots in various sizes at costs that anyone can afford because value our motto that is: "Your bright bridge to ownership. Our head office is located at Kabati town just 100 meters from the Embu-Nairobi highway. For more information Visit our Contacts Section.
